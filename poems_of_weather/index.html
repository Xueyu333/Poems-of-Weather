Sunny Day

The sun arrives with open hands,
It lifts the light from where I stand.

Foggy Day

The world dissolves in silver air,
Steps are lost—but I don’t care.

Cloudy Day

The sky wears gray like heavy wool,
A day half-bright, and never full.

Snowy Day

Snowflakes dance on silent ground,
Soft and slow, they make no sound.

Windy Day

Snowflakes dance on silent ground,
Soft and slow, they make no sound.
